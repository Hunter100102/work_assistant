⚙️ ETA Case Handling (Priority 5)
For GLC Cases or any Special Models (e.g. Maybach, AMG, G-Wagon, EQ, new models 190/197/199/117/167), escalate automatically with PRM.

If ETA < 2 weeks: No escalation; provide ETA.

If ETA > 2 weeks: Create a Shortage Query.

If follow-up date worsens or exceeds 4 weeks → Create SE and convert to CP.

✅ Always Check:
YMEIV: Available or inbound stock

Parts Locator: D2D flag (D2D eligible = contact CPM to process)

YPIM: Check for substitutes

Equal subs (A-type): Have dealer order

Non-equal subs (AE/RE): Send to Parts Technical for VIN fit

Remanufactured parts: Suffix 80/83/87 → check “New” versions and vice versa

Color-coded parts: e.g., suffix "9999" → Check for alternates
