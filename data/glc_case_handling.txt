TITLE: GLC Case Handling Guide

📌 When to Create a GLC Case
- For standard ETA delays over 2 weeks
- When the PO shows no updated confirmation after 14 days
- For Maybach, AMG, or G-Wagon orders (special vehicles) — escalate automatically

✅ Steps:
1. Check YPIM for substitute parts or stock
2. If ATP exists, send outbound and close case
3. If no ATP, create Shortage Escalation (SE)
4. Escalate to CP if follow-up date > 4 weeks

💬 Sample Comments:
- "ETA remains unchanged > 4 weeks, creating SE and escalating to CP."
- "GLC order for special vehicle. Escalating via PRM per SOP."

🚨 Do NOT escalate if:
- ATP exists and delivery is confirmed
- VIN is missing and dealer is unresponsive
- It's a stock order without urgency

📎 Tools Used:
- YPIM
- SO+
- XHPI
- DView (for shipment and invoice tracking)
- YME10 (for escalation)
